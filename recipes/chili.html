Simple Chili Recipe

When the weather gets cold, nothing beats Grandma's 5 Step Chili

Ingridents Required

  • 1 can of Corn
  • 1 can of Diced Tomatos
  • 1 Pound of Cooked & Diced Chicken Breast
  • 1 Pound of Cooked & Diced Ground Beef
  • 1 can of Mixed Beans.

How to Prepare and Cook Your Chili:

First turn on your crock-pot to the maximum heat. From there, combine all five ingridents and cook for two hours. After two hours set to low heat.
