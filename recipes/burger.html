Simple Burger Recipe

Do you want to make a simple burger? Well this is what we're going to be creating. Nothing beats a simple burger on a summer afternoon!

Ingridents Required

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Pepper
  • 1 Pound Ground Beef
  • 1 Set of Burger Buns

How to Prepare and Cook Your Burger:

Prepare your grill and preheat the grill. From there you will need to whisk together the egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl. After that, combine your mixture with your ground beef and mix thoroughly. From there form your patties to be about 3/4 of an inch thick. Next, cook up your hamburger for 6 minutes on each side. Lastly, add your desired toppings to your bun and add your burger and enjoy!
