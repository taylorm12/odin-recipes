Simple Shrimp Recipe

When the weather gets cold, nothing beats Grandma's 5 Step Chili

Ingridents Required

  • 1 Pound of Shrimp, de-tailed.
  • 1 Clove of Garlic, minced
  • 4 Tablespoons of Honey
  • 2 Tablespoons of Soy Sauce

How to Prepare and Cook Your Shrimp:

Combine together the soy sauce, honey, and garlic. Marinate the shrimp with the mixture for 30 minutes. Sear the shrimp in some oil until browned. Serve and enjoy.
